STUMP GRINDING

We offer our stump grinding services with various options to suite our clients need from grinding the main stump 225mm below the surface to the grinding out of the whole stump including chasing the buttress roots out to an agreed distance. Removing of the residue created for the grinding can also be removed from site and back filled with soil ready for grass seeding of creating a new planting area.

Emergency Call Outs

We offer an emergency call out service for those times when the mother nature reminds us of her power. We work on the the pretence of making the site safe and clearing if time permits or coming back at a later date to clear and tidy.
